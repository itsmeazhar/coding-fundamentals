MOST COMMON PET IN MALAYSIA

Pet Behavior Picture
Dog Communicate with humans, showing respect, empathy, fear, or frustration. Some common dog behaviors and what they mean are panting, licking, barking, and digging.
Cat Cat behavior includes body language, elimination habits, aggression, play, communication, hunting, grooming, urine marking, and face rubbing
Fish Complex and often change as a fish grows and matures. Behaviors are also influenced by changes in the environment. Fish react not only to other organisms, but also to their habitats.
